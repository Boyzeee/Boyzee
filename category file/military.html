TOP MILITARY ANIME

Military anime is a genre that explores armed conflict, military organizations, and the experiences of soldiers. It features themes such as duty, honor, camaraderie, and the realities of war. The genre can vary from historical dramas to futuristic settings, often delving into the psychological effects of combat. Popular examples include "Attack on Titan," "Fullmetal Alchemist: Brotherhood," and "Code Geass."
